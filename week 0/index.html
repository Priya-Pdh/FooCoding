Momo!! Nepalese Favorite cuisine

Picture of Jhol Momo

Momo is a type of steamed dumpling with some form of filling it can be either non-veg or veg. It has become a traditional delicacy in Nepal, Tibet, as well as among Nepalese and Tibetan communities in Bhutan.Traditionally, there used to be only steam momo but nowadays people have been creative with it, as a result there are varieties of momo available today in Nepal. For instace; Fried momo, veg momo, chicken momo, kothey momo, jhol momo, sadeko momo, etc.More info
